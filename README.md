# It's a Small World

It’s a Small World is a project based on stupidness. The stupidness of how easily the world is interconnected, and how stupidly I can connect myself to some of the most famous people on Earth through just a handful of connections. The theory of six degrees of separation is also affirmed in this project, with not a single connection out of the over 300 ever stretching onto the 7th degree of connection.

**Live Site:** https://ayaanahmedstudios.github.io/small-world/
